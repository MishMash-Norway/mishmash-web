MishMash is governed and managed by several organizational bodies, each with specific responsibilities and roles:

- [Management](management/) — Day-to-day management and coordination of activities
- [Work Package Leader Group](wp-leaders/) — Leads of the seven work packages
- [Board](board/) — Governance and decision-making body
- [Council](council/) — Strategic oversight and partner compliance
- [Scientific Advisory Board](scientific-advisory-board/) — International scientific guidance and evaluation
- [Stakeholder Board](stakeholder-board/) — Representatives from associated partner organisations and stakeholders
