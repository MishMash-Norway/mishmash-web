## Author affiliations

Since MishMash is not an organizational entity, you should not use a MishMash affiliation in publications but use your regular institution. To acknowledge your connection to MishMash, add a funding acknowledgment (see below).

## MishMash funding acknowledgment

Use something like this to in the acknowledgment section or as a footnote in papers and other related publications/artworks:

> This research was partially supported by the Research Council of Norway through the MishMash Centre for AI and Creativity, Project Number 357438.

## NVA registrations

For researchers reporting using NVA (the Norwegian Research Information Repository), all research results should be registered using the [MishMash project ID in NVA](https://nva.sikt.no/projects/2744839) (Project ID 2744839). Note that the NVA ID links to the RCN project number, making it easy to report correctly to RCN.

## Using the visual profile

Feel free to use MishMash's [visual profile elements](https://github.com/MishMash-Norway/mishmash-web/tree/main/assets/images) if you like, including the [bubble emblem](https://github.com/MishMash-Norway/mishmash-web/blob/main/assets/images/bubbles/mishmash_bubbles1_notext.svg). Again, since MishMash is it not a formal organization, these elements are considered *emblems*. You should use them together with your institutional *logo*.
