Welcome to a webinar organized by [WP4](https://mishmash.no/wp4/) titled *Critical AI and Creative Practice* with Eryk Salvaggio.

## Abstract

What does it mean to have a critical relationship with AI in creative work? In this webinar, Eryk Salvaggio draws on personal practice to explore how questioning AI tools can lead to more meaningful creative processes. The session closes with ways of thinking for educators who want to bring this perspective into their teaching.
 
## Bio

[Eryk Salvaggio](https://www.cdh.cam.ac.uk/about/people/eryk-salvaggio/) is a Gates Scholar at the University of Cambridge researching AI and the Digital Humanities, and a researcher and artist whose work explores the social and cultural impacts of artificial intelligence.
