Welcome to the official opening of MishMash Centre for AI and Creativity, where we explore the role of AI in art and the potential of art in AI. 

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/rq8UnZlzYk4?si=rjnfTaai8DHkRuPt" title="YouTube video player" frameborder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture; web-share" referrerpolicy="strict-origin-when-cross-origin" allowfullscreen></iframe>

The program features: 

- Sigrun Aasland, Minister of Research and Higher Education
- Anne Kjersti Fahlvik, Executive Director - Innovation in Industry and the Public Sector, Research Council of Norway
- Åse Wetås, Director of the National Library of Norway
- Øystein Strand, Director of Arts and Culture Norway
- Artistic performances by Victoria Johnson and Anders Tveit, Diego Marin and Benedikte Wallace, and Koka Nikoladze.
- Short talks and demonstrations by Synne Tollerud Bull, Daniel Formo and Olivier Lartillot
- Panels led by Daniel Nordgård and Ida Jahr

Right after the event, we will move over to Domus Bibliotheca for a reception.
