Welcome to the official opening of MishMash Centre for AI and Creativity. We are happy to welcome the Minister of research and higher education, Sigrun Aasland, Director of the Research Council of Norway, Mari Sundli Tveit, and the new Director of the National Library of Norway, Åse Wetås. 

The [program](https://www.uio.no/om/aktuelt/universitetsplassen/aulaen/arrangementer/litteratur-foredrag/2026/mishmash-ki-kreativitet.html) will consist of: 

- Artistic performances by Victoria Johnson and Anders Tveit, Diego Marin and Benedikte Wallace, and Koka Nikoladze.
- Demonstrations by Daniel Formo and Olivier Lartillot
- Panel discussions with 
- Short talks by Synne Tollerud Bull
- Panels led by Daniel Nordgård and Ida Jahr

Right after the event, we will move over to Domus bibliotheca for a reception.
