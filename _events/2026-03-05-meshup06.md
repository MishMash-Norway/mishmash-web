## Bio

[Dániel Péter Biró](https://www.danielpeterbiro.ca/) is Professor for Composition at the Grieg Academy, University of Bergen. He studied in Hungary, Switzerland, Germany and Austria and completed a PhD in composition at Princeton (2004); his research spans Hungarian folk music and Jewish and Islamic chant. His large cycles Mishpatim and Ethica (after Spinoza) have been commissioned and performed internationally by ensembles such as Ensemble SurPlus, ensemble recherche, Quasar and Arditti, and he has received numerous fellowships and awards including the Ernst von Siemens support, the Gigahertz Production Prize and a Guggenheim Fellowship (2017). He has held residencies at Radcliffe and the Experimentalstudio, received research funding from the Norwegian Artistic Research Programme (2021–2025), has recordings released or forthcoming on Neos and Genuin Classics, and his works are published by Edition Gravis.
