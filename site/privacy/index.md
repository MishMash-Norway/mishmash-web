This website does not use cookies. As simple as that. We do not set cookies in your browser, and we do not use third-party analytics or advertising tools that track visitors.

## Data classification

Each page on this site carries a data classification, shown in the "About the page" section of the footer. We follow the University of Oslo's four-level scheme: **green** (open), **yellow** (restricted), **red** (confidential), and **black** (strictly confidential).

Our pages are classified **green (open)**. Green covers information that may or should be available to the general public, with no special access restrictions — for example public web pages and openly published material. It applies when the university or its partners are not harmed if the information is exposed to third parties. Note that an open classification still means that only people with the correct credentials can *change* the information, and that being open does not in itself grant unrestricted rights to reuse the content.

See the University of Oslo's [data classification guide](https://www.uio.no/english/services/it/security/lsis/data-classes.html) for the full definitions.
