Ingerine Dahl is a Norwegian violinist who has served as second concertmaster of the Stavanger Symphony Orchestra since 2004 and is an associate professor at the Department of Classical Music, Faculty of Performing Arts, University of Stavanger. Her work centres on innovation and interdisciplinary performance combining music, light, movement, and audience participation. She is the founder, CEO, and inventor of [Muzziball](/institutions/muzziball/), an interactive sensory ball used in healthcare and educational settings.

She leads the MishMash seed-funded project [Creative Health Technologies](/projects/creative-health-technologies/), exploring music, movement and human-centred interaction in dementia care.
