## Summary
This project organizes an international academic workshop to discuss how creative AI can improve decision-making in volatile and complex (VUCA) environments. The workshop addresses the contribution of AI to societal safety and security, as well as the pitfalls of autonomous technology in crisis management. The resulting research agenda and conceptual paper will provide a foundation for empirical studies on AI usage in environments where improvisation and creativity are essential to solving global crises.
