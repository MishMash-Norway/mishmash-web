## Summary

The MishMash Mesh builds on the MishMash website with a digital platform that demonstrates AI as a catalyst for co-creativity, human agency, and interdisciplinary connection. For the consortium, an internal co-creative engine maps the expertise and work-package affiliations of members through a Model Context Protocol interface, letting members query the system in natural language and receive collaboration recommendations across work packages. For the public, a custom LLM interface grounded in MishMash documentation through strict vector search, together with a glossary and a WCAG 2.2-compliant accessibility toolbar, demystifies AI by showcasing tangible, human-centric use cases in art, education and health. The team also includes UI/UX designer Mihaela Daniela Crudu (INN), and the resulting codebase will be published open source on GitHub. See also [The MishMash Website](/projects/the-mishmash-website/).
