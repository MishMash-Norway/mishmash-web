## Summary

This project supports Hanne Maren Meldahl's participation in the seed-funded workshop initiative [KI i kunstnerisk utdanning og praksis](/projects/ki-i-kunstnerisk-utdanning-og-praksis/), organised through the collaboration between Kristiania and the Oslo National Academy of the Arts. Her participation is connected to her PhD project *The Haptic Hacking Lab*, which investigates how pedagogical practices in arts teacher education can be developed through artistic, material-based, and technological experimentation within postdigital learning environments. She takes part in planning and workshop activities while collecting multimodal research material — documentation of creative processes, interviews, written reflections, and artistic artefacts — for her dissertation and a planned article on collaborative artistic and pedagogical experimentation with AI in higher arts education.
