## Summary
This pilot project develops a prototype that transforms linear books into interactive, AI-driven narrative systems for children and adolescents. Readers can engage in virtual dialogues with authors and book characters or use AI to create their own stories and music based on the book's world. Developed through workshops and student programming at INN, the prototype aims to extend Interpretation and imagination in reading while exploring how interactive storytelling can function as a future form of learning.
