summary: The University of Tromsø – The Arctic University of Norway is a state university
  in Norway and the world's northernmost university. Located in the city of Tromsø,
  Norway, it was established by an act of parliament in 1968 as Norway's fourth university,
  and opened in 1972 as University of Tromsø. As of 2024, it is the largest research
  and educational institution in Northern Norway and the fifth-largest university
  in Norway. The university's location makes it a natural venue for the development
  of studies of the region's natural environment, culture, and society.
