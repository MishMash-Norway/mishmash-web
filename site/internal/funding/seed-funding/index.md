MishMash provides seed funding to support collaborative activities among partners. The plan is to announce seed funding calls several times per year, so no rush, there will be new opportunities. 

## Calls

- [Seed funding #1](/internal/funding/seed-funding/seed01/) – Deadline 2 March 2026
- [Seed funding #2](/internal/funding/seed-funding/seed02/) – Deadline 1 June 2026

## Assessment

The applications will be read by the work package leaders who, together with the management team, will make a decision. We employ a soft partiality check, meaning that WPL/management members will not evaluate applications where they have a direct conflict of interest.
