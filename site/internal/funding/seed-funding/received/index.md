## I have received seed funding from MishMash. Now what?

If you have received an email notifying you that your application for seed funding from MishMash has been approved, you may start working on the project. Before you begin, please note the following:

- You must carry out the project in accordance with the project description and budget submitted with the application. If there are changes to the project, notify us by email as they occur. Minor changes that do not affect the core objectives of the project are normally approved. Major changes that significantly alter the project's direction must be approved by the leadership group.
- Grants are paid retrospectively. This means you/your institution must cover project expenses up front and then request reimbursement from MishMash afterwards.
- As soon as the project is complete, you must submit a report confirming that the project was carried out in accordance with the project description and budget. The report must include a link to the project output — for example a published report, blog post, article, code, dataset, artwork, or other research outputs. [The report form can be found here](https://nettskjema.no/a/638628).
- If you wish to decline the grant, notify us by email. The funds will then be made available for other projects.

Seed funding from MishMash is normally disbursed retrospectively after the project is completed. The grant reimburses actual expenses and is not a lump-sum payment.

Procedures are continuously being developed and may not suit all MishMash partners equally well. If you have suggestions for improvements, qualifications, or special arrangements, we welcome your input.

## Budget partners

If your institution is a MishMash budget partner, follow these steps:

- If relevant: inform the financial officer at your institution that you have received seed funding from MishMash. The approval letter can be used as supporting documentation in the accounts.
- All goods and services needed for the project (travel, licenses, speaker fees, etc.) should be procured according to your institution's normal procedures.
- Use the procurement agreements that apply at your institution.
- When requesting reimbursement from your institution, indicate that the expenditures relate to the MishMash-funded project. For example, you can mark the reimbursement claim with the project ID shown in the approval letter. Alternatively, consult your institution about the best way to distinguish between regular MishMash expenses and seed-funding expenses.
- Project expenses must be included when the institution invoices MishMash/UiO during the year, either as a separate invoice or as part of a consolidated invoice. Expenses related to the seed-funded project must be clearly labeled with the project ID (as stated in the approval letter) and the text: "Work package 1, seed funding from MishMash."
- Submit a report using [this form](https://nettskjema.no/a/638628).
- Unused funds will not be disbursed. They will be returned to MishMash's operating budget to benefit other projects.

## Associated partners

If your institution is an associated partner that has signed the associated partner agreement and the funding agreement, follow the funding agreement when you receive seed funding from MishMash. In addition, please follow these instructions:

- All goods and services needed for the project (travel, licenses, speaker fees, etc.) should be procured according to your institution's normal procedures.
- When requesting reimbursement from your institution, indicate that the expenditures relate to the MishMash-funded project. For example, you can mark the reimbursement claim with the project ID shown in the approval letter.
- When the project is complete, submit a report using [this form](https://nettskjema.no/a/638628). The accounts must show exactly how much of the allocated funds have been spent. This information will be used as the basis for MishMash issuing a purchase order to your institution.
- The invoice must be labeled with the project ID (as stated in the approval letter) and the text: "Work package 1, seed funding from MishMash."
- Unused funds will not be disbursed. They will be returned to MishMash's operating budget to benefit other projects.

## Partners at UiO

If you are employed at UiO, follow these steps:

- All goods and services needed for the project (travel, licenses, speaker fees, etc.) should be procured according to UiO's normal procedures.
- Use the procurement agreements that apply at UiO.
- When requesting reimbursement from your department, indicate that the expenditures relate to the MishMash-funded project. For example, you can mark the reimbursement claim with the project ID shown in the approval letter.
- Submit a report using [this form](https://nettskjema.no/a/638628).
- Your department must submit an internal invoice to MishMash to obtain reimbursement. Send the following information to the project economist (or the person responsible for internal invoicing at your department):
  - Account: your project account
  - Cost code: 14400600
  - Project number: 106485101
  - Work package: Work package 1 (seed funding from MishMash)
  - Amount: xx xxx (the amount to be refunded)
- Unused funds will not be disbursed. They will be returned to MishMash's operating budget to benefit other projects.
