The formal establishment is continuing. After the MishMash Council was appointed last week, they have now appointed the following members for the MishMash Board:

  • Sunniva Whittaker (University of Agder) (leader)
  • Christian Blom (Notam – Norwegian Centre for Technology, Art and Music)
  • Christian Schüssler (Reimagine)
  • Hege Stensrud Høsøien (National Library of Norway)
  • Tine Grieg Viig (Western Norway University of Applied Sciences)
  • Alexander Refsum Jensenius (University of Oslo) (observer)
  • Eskil Muan Sæther (University of Oslo) (secretary)

The Board will oversee MishMash operations and be central in steering the centre’s development.
