Synne Tollerud Bull

Professor, School of Arts, Design, and Media, Kristiania University College

MishMash role: Member · WP2 , WP5 , WP6

Synne Tollerud Bull is a media artist, researcher, and Professor, currently serving as Pro-Dean of Research at the School of Arts, Design, and Media at Kristiania University of Applied Sciences. Her work bridges theory and practice to examine how emerging imaging technologies such as drones and AI shape societal structures through mediation in art and visual culture.
