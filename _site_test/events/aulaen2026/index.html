MishMash Opening Ceremony

8 April 2026, 18:00 –21:00 CEST — Aulaen, University of Oslo — Add to calendar

Welcome to the official opening of MishMash Centre for AI and Creativity, where we explore the role of AI in art and the potential of art in AI.

Program

Introductions

  • Rector Ragnhild Hennum (UiO)
  • Minister of Research and Higher Education Sigrun Aasland

Artistic and Scholarly Contributions

  • Flytpunkt 1: Victoria Johnson (Barratt-Due and UiO) and Anders Tveit (NMH)
  • Dancing Embryo: Diego Marin (UiO) and Benedikte Wallace (UiO)
  • Video Percussion: Koka Nikoladze (NMH)
  • Artistic Intelligence: Synne Tollerud Bull (Kristiania)
  • Rhythm and AI: Daniel Formo (NTNU)
  • Hardingfele and AI: Joan Gatti (NMH) and Olivier Lartillot (UiO) (in English)

Panel Discussion: Industry in Concern

  • Andrew Melchior
  • Øystein Strand (Arts and Culture Norway)
  • Nina Frederikke Grünfeld (INN)
  • MishMash Deputy Director Daniel Nordgård (UiA)

Panel Discussion: Knowledge, Culture, and AI

  • National Librarian Åse Wetås
  • Anne Kjersti Fahlvik, The Research Council of Norway
  • Professor Helge Jordheim, Director Centre for Global Sustainability (UiO)
  • MishMash Deputy Director Ida Jahr (INN)

Right after the event, we will move over to Domus Bibliotheca for a reception.
